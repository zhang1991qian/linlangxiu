Business Scope
Specialized in "Professional Transportation of Chemicals and Hazardous Chemicals"
Air Freight Export
Our company maintains excellent relationships with major airlines and airport cargo terminals, and has signed space charter agreements with some airlines. We guarantee cargo space with economic stability, and can provide services for overweight/overlength cargo, dangerous goods, and temperature-controlled cargo.
Ocean Freight Export
With years of port operation experience, we have extensive connections in port areas and long-term contracted cooperation with major shipping lines, able to provide FCL (Full Container Load) and LCL (Less than Container Load) services.
Ocean & Air Freight Import
Leveraging our comprehensive global agent network, we can pick up goods from overseas factories, complete customs declaration, and arrange shipping and flights to transport goods to China.
Comprehensive Agent Network
We provide DAP/DDP/EXW/FOB services. Our overseas department colleagues are responsible for seamless communication with our global partners, and will communicate with foreign parties in a timely manner day and night to ensure that cargo transportation is not delayed due to time difference issues.
Dangerous Goods Sample Export
For the export of dangerous goods samples, in addition to regular air freight export, we have also developed a dedicated dangerous goods line service via Hong Kong Fedex and DHL.
Air Cold Chain Transportation
Our company has dedicated personnel responsible for cold chain air freight export business, with constant temperature cold storage at the airport, providing door-to-door dry ice and blue ice export services throughout the entire process.

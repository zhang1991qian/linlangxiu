Air Freight Export

Our company maintains excellent relationships with major airlines and airport cargo terminals, and has signed space board agreements with some airlines, ensuring cargo space availability and offering preferential pricing. We have our own customs declaration, inspection declaration and cargo delivery teams. Familiar with the routes, advantages and disadvantages of major airlines, we leverage our professional knowledge and experience to select cost-effective flights for customers, meeting the diverse needs of different clients. Our consistent and unremitting efforts have equipped us with all the prerequisites to become a first-class air freight service provider.

The air freight export services we can provide for customers include:

Air transportation consulting and planning, selecting appropriate airlines and routes for customers.

Acting as agent for chemical identification and hazardous goods classification identification, etc. (You only need to send the required documents and samples to us, and we will coordinate with the identification institutions, saving your company from the cumbersome processes of communication and sample delivery with identification institutions.)

Re-palletizing and reinforcement, label affixing, sorting or repackaging, photography services, etc. For some dangerous goods, our UN-certified packaging can be used to exempt the need for dangerous goods packaging certificates, enabling safe, fast and efficient transportation of dangerous goods shipments.

For dangerous goods shipments, we can prepare dangerous goods declaration documents on behalf of customers and affix various dangerous goods labels in accordance with dangerous goods regulations.

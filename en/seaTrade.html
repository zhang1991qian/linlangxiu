Ocean Freight Export

As a Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC), Linlangxiu has cooperative relationships with multiple shipping lines, ensuring cargo space availability and competitive pricing. We are committed to providing a full range of import and export ocean freight services, such as FCL/LCL export, warehouse stuffing, container trucking to port, customs declaration, etc. With years of port operation experience, we have extensive connections in port areas. In case of tight cargo space with shipping lines, we can provide container tracking and loading supervision services, effectively ensuring the on-time shipment of goods.

The ocean freight export services we can provide for customers include:

Ocean freight transportation consultation and route planning, selecting suitable shipping lines and routes for customers.

Agent for chemical identification and hazardous characteristics classification identification, etc. (You only need to send the required documents and samples to us, and we will coordinate with the identification institutions, saving your company from the cumbersome processes of communication and sample delivery with identification institutions.)

We have stuffing warehouses at Yangshan Port and Waigaoqiao Port, providing services such as container stuffing, container drop-off, pre-port entry, as well as reinforcement, palletizing, labeling, repackaging, photography and other services. For dangerous goods stuffing, we can also provide services such as warehouse certification for pick-up and ship-side direct loading.

We can provide door-to-door container trucking from factories to port, with stable and reliable container truck fleet suppliers.

We also have rich experience in tank container export, and can provide dangerous goods tank container export services from Shanghai Port and Ningbo Port.

About Us

Company Profile

Shanghai Linlangxiu Supply Chain Management Co., Ltd. was established in 2022. Since its inception, the company has been focusing on the "professional transportation of chemical products and hazardous chemicals". Leveraging professional expertise, we provide "safe, professional and efficient" services for chemical enterprises, ensuring worry-free import & export transportation and customs clearance for them.

We possess professional experience in sea and air freight as well as import & export services for chemicals and dangerous goods, along with strictly trained and well-qualified staff, which guarantees the safe and timely delivery of customers' goods to all parts of the world.

With abundant resources, we maintain sound agency cooperation with major airlines, shipping companies, government authorities, ports, airport terminals and other entities, which serves as a guarantee for offering competitive prices to our customers.

Holding NVOCC qualifications and air cargo sales qualifications, the company has established a comprehensive global overseas network, enabling it to provide clients with a variety of effective solutions such as DAP, DDP, EXW, and FOB to meet diverse needs. Our core competitiveness includes:

Professional service team providing flexible and diversified service solutions with competitive price advantages

Excellent and experienced ground operation team

Long-term good cooperative relations with customs and inspection & quarantine authorities

Providing real-time cargo tracking service and cargo transportation insurance service

With a comprehensive global agency network, our company can pick up goods from overseas factories, complete customs declaration, and then arrange shipping vessels and flights to transport the goods to China. Our customs declaration and inspection team consists of experienced senior customs declarers and senior commodity inspection specialists, who maintain good relations with customs and commodity inspection authorities, ensuring high efficiency in customs clearance. We can also assist customers in HS code classification and propose optimized customs clearance processes based on the characteristics of various goods. Reasonable customs planning can help customers reduce tariff expenditures and lower costs.

Our operators track the voyage and flight information of inbound goods in a timely manner, monitor the status of imported goods, and notify customers of inbound information at the first time. We handle all transportation agency business of imported goods and complete all procedures after the arrival of imported goods in accordance with customers' entrustment.

We have our own customs supervised warehouse at the airport. For imported goods with large tonnage or customers who have signed long-term import agreements with our company, we can reduce or exempt part of the warehousing fees.

For the import of dangerous goods, we are familiar with the import operation process of dangerous goods and the relevant requirements of customs and government departments on dangerous goods. We can assist customers in conducting classification identification, label identification, applying for 3C exemption and coating filing on their behalf, as well as designing Chinese labels for customers. We handle or review relevant documents for customers before the goods are shipped to avoid additional costs after arrival at the port.

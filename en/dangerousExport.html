Dangerous Goods Sample Export

For the export of dangerous goods samples, in addition to regular air freight export services, our company has also developed a dedicated dangerous goods line service via Hong Kong Fedex and DHL. The advantages of using the Hong Kong dangerous goods express line are as follows:

No chemical identification certificate is required, only English MSDS (Material Safety Data Sheet) is needed. For shipments with multiple product names in one consignment, the cost of chemical identification alone is RMB 500 per product name, making domestic air freight cost-prohibitive. The Hong Kong dangerous goods express model can save identification fees.

No customs clearance document or pesticide export license is required. The HS codes of some products involve customs clearance documents and pesticide export licenses. Applying for such documents separately for small samples is time-consuming, laborious and uneconomical. Using the Hong Kong express line eliminates the need for these documents, ensuring high efficiency and simplicity.

Door-to-door service in the destination country is available. If dangerous goods samples are exported via domestic air freight, the consignee still needs to complete customs clearance and pick up the goods at the destination. The customs clearance and pickup fees for dangerous goods at the destination port are usually not low, starting from USD 200-300. Coupled with air freight charges and domestic dangerous goods handling fees, the overall cost is very high. The consignee also has to provide relevant customs clearance documents, which is troublesome and labor-intensive. In contrast, our Hong Kong dangerous goods express line offers much more competitive pricing and door-to-door delivery service, saving the consignee time and effort.
